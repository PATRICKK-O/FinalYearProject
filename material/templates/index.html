📘 About STUDEX

The Personalized Study Material Recommender System (PSMRS) is built to simplify your academic life.

We provide access to carefully sourced PDFs and videos from trusted platforms like online textbook archives and educational content from YouTube. Our system ensures that what you see is relevant to your academic interests using adaptive technology that continuously learns how best to support your goals.
